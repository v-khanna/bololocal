# HearIt Privacy Policy

HearIt does not collect, transmit, store, or share any user data.

## What stays on your Mac

- Every piece of text you select is processed locally on your Mac.
- All speech synthesis runs locally via Apple's MLX framework on the Neural Engine / GPU.
- Your settings (selected voice, speed, hotkey, launch-at-login) live in macOS UserDefaults on your Mac. They are never transmitted.

## The one network call HearIt ever makes

On first launch, HearIt downloads the Qwen3-TTS voice model file (~500 MB) from Hugging Face. After that, **no network calls happen — ever**. You can verify this with Little Snitch or any network monitor.

## What HearIt does NOT do

- No analytics
- No telemetry
- No "anonymous usage data"
- No crash reporting
- No user accounts
- No sync
- No advertising IDs
- No tracking

## Accessibility access

HearIt requests macOS Accessibility access (granted in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility) so it can read the text you've selected in other apps. This permission stays on your Mac — it does not enable any kind of monitoring or transmission.

## Questions

This is a single-developer indie app. For questions or concerns, contact the developer through Setapp or via the repository.
